About

I graduated from Parsons the New School for Design with a BFA in Fashion Design in 2014. Since then, I’ve accepted commissions and developed my portfolio across a range of media, including UX design, fashion design & construction, styling, illustration, graphic design, textile methods (weaving, felting, knitting), baking, writing, millinery, hand beading, and dog petting. As a tenacious worker with varied work experience across several departments -- marketing, merchandising, production, and design -- I am a remarkably adaptable employee who can flourish in a variety of environments, whether structured or unstructured, team-based or independent. I am able to pick up new skills quickly and to adeptly apply my current skills to many different tasks and positions. I am a quick learner, looking forward to enhancing my skills and growing within the UX field.
